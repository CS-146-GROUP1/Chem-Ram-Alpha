They see me through different eyes

Many of the books that I have read in my life that had something to do with the American experience through the lens of a minority lens talked about how they tried to adjust to the conditions of the new land that they had now accepted as their home. While I am sure there are other books that talk about the American or other developed country experience from the point of view of someone who was born here but not someone who is caucasian, I haven’t seen any.
